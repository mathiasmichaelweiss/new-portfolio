Solar Sustem project

One of my first projects towards mastering web development. I was inspired by my love for space and everything connected with it. I tried to do this project mostly in JavaScript, I also used the Sass preprocessor, trying to use HTML as little as possible, because I needed to consolidate my knowledge of these technologies. In it I used such technologies as classes, methods of iterating over arrays and pseudo-arrays, worked with styles inside JavaScript.
